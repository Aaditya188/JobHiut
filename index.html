F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where do I place the order for services?

    You can first go to the main page and then you can login in by clicking on the the login button or sign up if you don't have an account. After this step you can select the avail services option and then fill up the form according to your need. Here you will get the option for either of the three services.

  • Where do I add special instructions for the food I order?

    When you fill of the form for tiffin services, you will get a box to enter special instructions. Here you can write the kind of changes in the food you require.

  • How do I apply to get a job?

    when you go to the login option and login into your account you will get a "I want to work" option. There you can fill the form according to the position on which you want to apply. You can work as a manager or as a worker for us.

  • Where can I get the price of the maid services for the work I choose?

    when you fill the form for the maid services you can enter the services you require in the given box and we will get back to you on your mail along with the prices and then you can select the ones you wish to apply for.
